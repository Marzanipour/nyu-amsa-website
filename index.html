Established in 1950, the American Medical Student Association is the oldest and largest independent association of physicians-in-training in the United States, with students participating internationally.

Our chapter

Ambitious, supportive, and built for future healthcare leaders.

The AMSA New York University Chapter is a dynamic premedical organization open to all students passionate about medicine. Across the semester, we curate opportunities in career development, professional exploration, and community building.

Through conversations with distinguished healthcare professionals, practical guidance, hands-on experiences, and peer support, we help students move through the demanding pre-medical track with more clarity and confidence.

We embrace AMSA's broader mission by building a community that thinks seriously about medical education, leadership, and the social, moral, and ethical obligations of the medical profession.
